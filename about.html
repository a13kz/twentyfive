About:

Teratoma is a genre-blending band based in Gothenburg, Sweden. Drawing inspiration from indie, punk, pop, rock, and hip-hop, the group creates a unique and energetic sound that reflects the diversity of its influences. Known for their dynamic live shows and raw, honest songwriting, Teratoma mixes catchy melodies with gritty rhythms and sharp lyrics. Formed by a group of friends with different musical backgrounds, the band focuses on breaking down genre boundaries and experimenting with sound while staying grounded in strong songwriting. Whether it’s a high-energy punk track or a laid-back hip-hop beat, Teratoma brings emotion, attitude, and originality to everything they do.
